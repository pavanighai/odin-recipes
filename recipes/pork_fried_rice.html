Pork Fried Rice

Description

This healthy yet delicious dinner is sure to fill you up. It covers many of the food groups including meats (the pork), grains (rice), and vegetables (carrots, peas, broccoli, etc.).

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon of butter
  • 1 (6 ounce) boneless pork loin chop, cut into small pieces
  • 1 chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup of chopped broccoli
  • 1 large beaten egg
  • 1 cup of cold cooked rice
  • 1/4 cup of frozen peas
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground ginger

Steps

  1. In a large non-stick skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add pork, green onion, carrot, and broccoli; cook and stir until pork is cooked through. This should take 7 to 10 minutes.
  2. Put the pork mixture in a bowl and return the skillet to medium heat. In the skillet, stir an egg and scramble until completely set.
  3. Add the pork mixture back into the skillet; stir in rice, peas, soy sauce, garlic powder, and ground ginger.
  4. For 7 to 10 minutes, cook and stir until heated through. Serve and enjoy!
