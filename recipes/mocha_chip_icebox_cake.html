Mocha Chip Icebox Cake

Description

This quick and easy cake will melt in your mouth the second you bite into it. It is filled with both cookies and a soft, creamy mocha mousse that is guerenteed to lift your spirits. This cake is the perfect combination of cookies, caffeine, and cake!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of white sugar
  • 1 teaspon of instant coffee powder
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 2 cups cold heavy cream
  • 12 ounces mascarpone cheese or cream cheese
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages double chocolate chip cookies

Steps

  1. Whisk or beat sugar, coffee powder, cocoa powder, vanilla, coffee liqueur, and cream in a mixing bowl until the mixture gets thick but before soft peaks form. Beat the mascarpone into the mixture just until stiff peaks form (don't mix any furthur or the cream will start to seperate). Refrigirate the mocha cream until it is ready for use.
  2. Take a 9-inch springform pan and line it with a round peice of parchment paper, however, this step is optional.
  3. In the bottom of the pan, place 8 of the double chocolate cookies and then break a 9th cookies and fill in the gaps. Spread 1/4 of the mocha cream evenly on the cookies. Press more cookies lighty into the filling and add another 1/4 layor of cream on top to create more layers. Continue doing the same process until all the ingredients run out (5 cookie layers and 4 cream layers) or to your desired layers. Wrap and chill the cake for 12 to 24 hours
  4. Once the cake had chilled and ready to serve, take a thin knife and gently run it through the edge of the cake and the inside springform pan ring. Release the spring and slide the cake off gently, slice, and serve.
